Rain Alert App Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 10, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Rain Alert ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our mobile application Rain Alert (the "App").

Information We Collect

When you use Rain Alert, we collect the following types of information:

  • Location Data: We collect your device's location to provide you with local weather alerts. This includes GPS coordinates when the app is running and in the background.
  • Device Information: We collect information about your device, including device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you use the App, including alert interactions, app settings, and features you use.
  • Alert Feedback: When you provide feedback about the accuracy of weather alerts, we collect this information to improve our service.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide accurate, location-based weather alerts for rain and freezing conditions
  • Improve the App's algorithms and notification accuracy
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance app functionality
  • Monitor app performance and diagnose technical issues
  • Develop new features based on user behavior and feedback

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We share your data with:

  • Weather Data Providers: We access the U.S. National Weather Service API to retrieve weather information, but we do not share your personal information with them.
  • Firebase: We use Google Firebase for analytics and crash reporting. Your data is handled according to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices

You can control your data in the following ways:

  • You can adjust location permissions through your device settings
  • You can disable notifications through your device settings
  • You can customize alert thresholds and frequency in the app settings
  • You can uninstall the App at any time, which will stop all data collection

Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
